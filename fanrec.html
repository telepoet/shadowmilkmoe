Recommended Fanworks

Want some fics to read but don't know where to start? Here's some of my favorites:
  • Chiaroscuro - Cinnamoncandyy
    100k+ words - shadowvanilla - redemption arc, canon-divergent
    • Now, I tend to dislike Beast redemption arc works for the myriad mischaracterization pitfalls that tend to come with them, but not only is Chiaroscuro's twist on the premise incredibly on the nose, but the plot progression throughout remains snappy without compromising on buildup. The variety of character dynamics that get explored outside of the primary slash pairing keeps things refreshing, and the plural representation is a super intriguing take on Shadow Milk's character that's handled with tact. It should be telling that this is the primary fic on this list that I've already completed multiple rereads of.
  • From the Spire to a Peak - FaeMytho
    100k+ words - shadowvanilla, truthlessage - crossover au, "swap" au, slow burn
    • Wherein FaeMytho launches canon SMPV and Diverged SGTR at each other like Barbie dolls to some mildly terrifying results; Spirepeak remains far and away my favorite interpretation of both the Sage of Truth and Truthless Recluse in the entire fandom, and that's not even getting into the series of misunderstandings that rapidly cascades into the worlds funniest love square. An extremely well executed plotline with some great worldbuilding and tons of fun Deceit Trio & Bravegang content inbetween.
  • #shadownilla - thegoldenappleofdiscord
    10k+ words - shadowvanilla - outsider POV, mixed media, crackfic
    • This is genuinely the funniest fucking fanfic I've ever read.
  • Echo Paradox* - thebiscuitlabryinth
    10k+ words - shadowvanilla - character study (written pre-beast yeast 7)
    • One of my favorite examples of Apollo's Gift of Prophecy in a fanwork, the entirety of Echo Paradox was written long before much of the dynamics and theories it touches upon were canonized. Ergo, this fic has a large smattering of speculative energy and canon divergence present, but I implore you to put yourself in the shoes of a 2023 era fandom member who got to see almost all of the subject matter here get actualized in the game a little under a year later. A fascinating time capsule into the fandom's premier approach to Shadow Milk's background and fixation upon his other half.
    (*There's an AO3 mirror of this work floating around if you prefer the convenience over a reverse-chronological tumblr tag, but I won't be linking to it here as it wasn't made by Fable herself.)
