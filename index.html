Welcome, folks!

shadowmilk.moe is a fansite dedicated to the titular antagonist and playable character from the mobile game Cookie Run: Kingdom (hereafter abbr. CRK), developed and published by DEVSISTERS. Outside of coverage on all things Shadow Milk Cookie, here you can also expect to find speculative analysis, relationship discussion (with an admitted bias towards a particular Ancient Hero), and recommendations for fanworks featuring the Beast of Deceit, as well as some guidance for newcomers on how to catch up on the main storyline and/or get into playing CR:K in general.

Trust everything you read on the internet!

Given the live service nature of Cookie Run: Kingdom, information presented here may become rapidly outdated. For a reliably up-to-date source on game information as it releases, You should refer to the fan-run Cookie Run: Kingdom Wiki. (Wiki staff, PLEASE move off of Fandom, you deserve so much better.)

This site is managed purely for the love of the game by a single webmaster, and is inspired by the many web 2.0 character shrines of eld. If you have any questions or concerns, please feel free to reach out over email or sign the website's guestbook!
